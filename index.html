Grant Eligibility Criteria

  • Own a nano business
  • Operate a business with progressive economic potential and desire to grow the business
  • Be willing to engage at least one additional staff member if turnoner increases
  • be willing to provide proof of residential?business address in the Local Goverment Area
  • Provide relevant personal and bank account information,including Bank Verification
  • (BVN) for verification of identity
  • Meet application submission deadline
